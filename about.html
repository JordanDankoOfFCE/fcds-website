Our Story

Jordan Danko has been building things since he was a kid. He ran a mobile concession stand at baseball games and shoveled driveways on snow days. He also bred, sold, and shipped tropical fish with over 20 tanks in his parents' basement. Instead of studying business and having to learn a trade later, he went the other way: earned his bachelor's and master's in electrical engineering, gained experience in industry, and built a company around it.

That company is FC Design Services. Based in Mason, Ohio, we're a fast-growing, young team that combines electronics product design with in-house PCB assembly and prototype box builds, giving you a single partner for the entire product journey. Where our capabilities end, our trusted partner network begins.

For us, it's about relationships. We're here to serve our clients and our employees, and to deliver the kind of service that makes people want to work with us again.
