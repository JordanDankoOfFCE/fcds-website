Our Services

Comprehensive electronics design and manufacturing services from concept through production.

Electronics Product Design

Electrical, firmware, and mechanical design from concept through production-ready files. Whether it's an IoT device, complex machine, or a simple sensor board, we have the expertise to bring your vision to life.

  • Schematic capture and PCB layout
  • Embedded firmware development
  • Mechanical enclosure and thermal design

PCB Assembly & Manufacturing

Prototype through mid-volume PCBA with full process management: parts procurement, assembly, inspection, and test under one roof.

  • Surface mount (SMT) and through-hole assembly
  • Functional testing
  • Design for manufacturability (DFM) review

Box Build Assembly

Complete system integration from sub-assemblies to finished, tested products ready for deployment.

  • Electro-mechanical and wire harness assembly
  • Enclosure integration and system-level testing
  • Labeling, packaging, and fulfillment

Custom Battery Packs

Custom battery solutions available through our trusted partner, from cell selection through pack design, assembly, and safety testing.

  • Li-ion, LiFePO4, and NiMH chemistries
  • Battery management system (BMS) design
  • Portable, industrial, medical, and military applications

R&D Support

We help you validate concepts, build prototypes, and transfer designs to production, reducing risk and accelerating time-to-market.

  • Feasibility studies and technology assessment
  • Proof-of-concept and rapid prototyping
  • Testing, characterization, and production transfer

Where our capabilities end, our trusted partner network begins.
